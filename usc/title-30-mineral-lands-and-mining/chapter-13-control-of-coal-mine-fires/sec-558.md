§558. Authorization of appropriations

There are hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums,[1](#558_1_target) as may be necessary to carry out the provisions and purposes of this chapter.

(Aug. 31, 1954, ch. 1156, §8, 68 Stat. 1011; Pub. L. 102–486, title XXV, §2504(d)(3), Oct. 24, 1992, 106 Stat. 3106.)

#### **Editorial Notes** ####

#### Amendments ####
